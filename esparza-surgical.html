SafeTuck ↗
Safe. Simple. Secure.

SafeTuck is an FDA-registered, reusable limb securement system that attaches directly to the OR bed rail. It replaces the outdated, time-consuming practice of sheet-tucking with a quick, one-person, one-minute solution that keeps patients safely secured throughout surgery.
