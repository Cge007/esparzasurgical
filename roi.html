Methodology & Assumptions

Conservative estimate includes: (1) full disposable cost elimination per case, (2) direct labor savings using staff cost rate × time freed × staff count reduction, and (3) 20% of recovered OR time valued at your stated rate — a standard conservative assumption used by healthcare finance teams when backfill is uncertain.

Full potential includes 100% of recovered OR time at your stated rate — valid when facilities have surgical backlogs or documented unmet demand. Always validate with your facility's actual OR utilization data.

SafeTuck reduces arm-tuck time from a facility average of 5–10 minutes to under 1 minute, and reduces required staff from 2–4 persons to 1. Disposable positioning materials (sheets, foam pads, tape) are fully replaced by the reusable device. Individual results vary by case mix, facility size, and current practices.
